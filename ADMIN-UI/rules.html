Rules & Policies

General Waste Collection

General waste should be placed in black bags and put out for collection by 7:00 AM on the designated collection day.

Recyclables Collection

Recyclables should be separated into blue bags for paper and green bags for plastics and metals. Collection occurs every Wednesday.

Hazardous Waste

Hazardous waste, including batteries and chemicals, should be taken to the designated drop-off points. Do not place these items in regular waste bins.

Bulk Waste

Bulk waste items such as furniture and appliances require a special pickup request. Contact the waste management office to schedule a pickup.

Penalties

Failure to comply with waste collection rules may result in fines. Ensure all waste is properly sorted and placed out on the correct days.
